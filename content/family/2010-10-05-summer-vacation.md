We went on a long weekend over labour day to Luray caverns and to Shenandoah national park.  The girls had a great time, and Jasmine started playing with the video camera.  We went to Luray caves and kayaking.  The girls were tired of the caves by the end of the tour, but they really liked to kayak...I think Libby's favorite part of the trip was the pizza place we found where they played [rock, scissors, paper](http://andyeick.com/blog/2010/09/25/rock-paper-scissors-friends/)....Sorry for the picture dump, I ran out of time to parse out the good ones!
